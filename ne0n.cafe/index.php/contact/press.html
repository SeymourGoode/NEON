Press Resources

NEON Media Badges will be released to individuals who meet the requirements for either a press pass or a staff photographer pass.  There is a limited number of these passes available, so please submit your request as early as possible!

Press Pass Requirements:

  • The organization (print, digital or otherwise) that you work for.
  • A link or copy of a published article in the last 12 months for verification.

Staff Photographer Requirements:

  • A list of previous events or media outlets where you have been a staff photographer in the last 24 months, with a link to your portfolio or published samples of your work.
  • A basic equipment list
  • Your availability schedule for the weekend of the event. You will be given two shifts of three hours each in exchange for your pass for the day. When not on an assigned shift, you are free to enjoy the event.
