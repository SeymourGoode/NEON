Join Us

NEON is looking for enthusiastic, reliable volunteers to help us make our first year a big success. Whether you're only available for the weekend itself, or whether you wish to be involved in the months leading up to it and be a part of this exciting new event at the ground level, we're interested in hearing from you!

Volunteering comes with both responsibilities and privileges; we offer a comped day pass for each 4-hour shift you volunteer per day (you may do up to 2 maximum per day), which means that you are free to enjoy the event when your shift is over. You will also receive a special staff t-shirt, meals, and access to the volunteer lounge as a home base.

Volunteering is a chance for budget-conscious and/or passionate, dedicated individuals to donate their time, make new friends, and become a part of the NEON team--it's also a great resume-builder. We have a limited number of volunteer spots available, and preference will be given to those who demonstrate motivation, professionalism, and accountability. If you have prior experience volunteering or working at events, please mention that as well. Thanks, and we hope you will join us in 2018!
