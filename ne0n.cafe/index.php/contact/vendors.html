The NEON Plaza Mall

Vending at NEON offers you the chance to showcase your goods directly to this highly selective and passionate group of people. NEON attendees have a keen eye for quality and authenticity. They appreciate niche content, but they also enjoy trying new things, which is why they had so much fun exploring NEON 2018’s many programming areas. By purchasing a booth at NEON, you become a part of an immersive retro experience unlike any other event in the world. The closer your brand is to the ethos and subject matter that NEON embodies, the more likely it is to appeal to our attendees; but you may also have the option of tailoring a specialized product or service that speaks to them directly as well.

NEON's focus areas for vendors retro clothing, classic video gaming, cult film, retro-related art, music, and more.

We’ve Got Options!
Vendor Booths: $200 and include 2 Vendor Passes. Your space will be 8ft x 8ft and include 1 6ft table and 2 chairs. Feel free to bring more tables and fixtures to fill up your space.

Artist Tables: $150 and include 2 Vendor Passes. Your space will be 1 6ft table with 2 chairs. You will not be permitted to bring extra tables.
