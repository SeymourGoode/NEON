Give Me Money

The official hotel for NEON is the Crowne Plaza Hotel.

It is located at 801 Greenwich Avenue, Warwick, RI 02886. Recently renovated in 2019, including all guest rooms, the Crowne Plaza is the immersive home of NEON 2020 in the heart of the Ocean State.   

A discounted Festival Room Block is Available! Book your room for NEON today by visiting http://www.crownehotelwarwick.com/ and using the room code "NEO" (CASE SENSITIVE!) when prompted, or call 401-732-6000 and mention you would like the NEON 2020 room rate. 

Event rooms are just $139.00 a night.  Book early, the block is expected to sell out quickly!

 

Crowne Plaza Features and Amenities

  • 3PM Check-In / 11 AM Check-Out (Late Check-Out Available)
  • Wireless Internet is available in Standard Rooms, Executive Rooms, Suites, Business Center, Public Areas.
  • 24 hour Business Center.
  • Complimentary Daily Self-Parking is available.
  • Free Shuttle Service from T.F. Green International Airport
  • Free Shuttle Service to/from overflow hotels and other affiliated hotels.
